Paneer curry

delicious and creamy Indian curry made with paneer

Recipe:Paneer curry

Ingredients:
250 grams paneer, cut into cubes 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or ghee 1 large onion, finely chopped 2 tomatoes, finely chopped 1 green bell pepper (capsicum), diced 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste 1 teaspoon cumin seeds 1 teaspoon coriander powder 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste) Salt to taste Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish
